DINING AT THE HOTEL

When it comes to using the freshest produce to create inspired and inventive dishes; we set the bar extremely high.
And in a place famous for the quality and abundance of its dining options; our reputation is second to none.



In Galle, fresh produce and seafood are available in abundance all year round; famous for our delectable lobster and jumbo prawns, we also have the luxury of being able to select from the widest range of the freshest fruit and vegetables. No surprise then that freshness forms the foundation of our food philosophy.


Our menu is quite literally a wonderful journey, since we draw inspiration from the famous Spice Route – the one merchants travelled from Europe to the mysterious lands of the East, in search of exotic spices.


Every dish that makes its aromatic way from our kitchen to your table, is also laced with that most precious ingredient – inspiration. We add that little something extra to every offering on our menu, so that, ever after, you will say “I wish they made it like the Galle Royal Hotel does”. Visit the Galle Royal Hotel and experience the cuisine of the best restaurants in Galle.


Galle Royal Hotel offers guests a wide range of dining options during their stay. Our multi cuisine restaurant serves all varieties of dishes to best suit your interest. Our guests can choose from a range of dishes that includes Italian, Western, Indian, Arabic, Chinese and our Signature Sri Lankan dishes all under one roof.


All our dishes are prepared by high profile culinary teams with worldwide experience and with a very unique and eye catching interior and décor. you are sure to enjoy your sophisticated evening.



THE PEACOCK

With contemporary decor and elegant setups, The Peacock creates a beautiful setting where guests can enjoy a pleasant and intimate dining experience. Dive into a beautiful showcase of glorious Sri Lankan flavours and sample other delectable cuisine from around the world at this beautifully appointed restaurant in Galle.



WADIYA BY RASA MALAYSIA

Standing out from other Galle beach restaurants, Wadiya by Rasa Malaysia restaurant offers authentic Malaysian cuisine. Tantalise your taste buds with a beautiful fusion of spicy, creamy, tangy and unforgettable flavours, texture and colours which will please and provide you with a wholesome and satisfying experience.



GARDEN BAR

Out of the many hotel bars in Galle, our Garden Bar is the perfect place to lounge away on a long afternoon and relax as the beautiful landscaped gardens in our courtyard lights up and day drifts effortlessly into night. Cool off with our extensive selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages and cool off while savouring each sip during the day or under the stars.



LOUNGE BAR

All for delivering enticing new experiences, our bartenders are constantly tapping into local flavours, interesting presentations and new fruity combinations to bring you special drinks and unique cocktails. One of the best bars in Galle, sit back and unwind with a chilled drink in your hand as you savour a scenic view overlooking the pool garden.
