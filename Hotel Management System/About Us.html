ABOUT THE GALLE ROYAL HOTEL

Simply put, the Galle Royal Hotel is one of the island’s best boutique hotels.
A deft combination of history, luxury and serenity, it is an ideal base from which to explore
this unique world heritage site.


One of the grand mansions of the fort, this iconic building has been many things to many people; a former gem merchants mansion, RAF barracks, a post office, bakery, lapidary and even a pitch for the forts juvenile cricketers; the Galle Royal Hotel is quintessential Galle, a beautiful, eclectic, eccentric gem of a place in the town that exerts a magnet appeal for history and culture buffs the world over.


In the decade following its brilliant restoration, this 11 suite boutique hotel has gained an impeccable reputation for offering a singular experience; paying deep homage to Galle’s rich cultural and aesthetic values, while combining luxurious surroundings and delectable cuisine with the highest standards of service excellence.


The perfect mix of culture, class and comfort, the Galle Royal Hotel is not to be missed; not just because it’s one of the best hotels in Galle, but because it is simply one of the best holiday experiences, ever.
